Choose Your Game!

By Alan Park

These are the three games you can play, and one virtual dice roller.

Rock-Paper-Scissors

Classic game where: Rock > Scissors > Paper > Rock ...

Chin Chin

Players raise 0, 1, or 2 thumbs. Each player takes a turn on what they think will be raised. If the player guesses correctly, they lose a thumb. First player to have no thumbs wins.

007

Players have the options of choosing: Load, Block, or Shoot. First player to get shot loses.

Dice Roller

Simple dice roller, with chosen amount of dice. Can be useful when no dice are on hand.
